ABOUT THE AUTHOR

SIR IKECHI AHAMEFULE NWOGU

PROFILE OF SIR IKECHI AHAMEFULE NWOGU

Sir Ikechi Ahamefule Nwogu is an author, highly sought-after keynote and motivational speaker, futurist, and a strategic advancement advisor to governments, organisations, people and businesses. A Corporate Governance and Administrative expert, he helps organizations improve their operational performance, manage finance more intelligently and optimise approaches to new results and growth methodologies.

Sir Ikechi Nwogu, Fellow of the Mentoring & Career Development Institute and Member of the Institute of Directors, Co-founded CINFORES Limited, a Tech-Resource and Educational Consultancy Company and is currently the sitting Director of Administration.

IKECHI NWOGU ANT

I am an ANT. By this I mean I am an Advocate for National Transformation. This new realization is chiefly inspired by my study of ants especially as instructed by God in Proverbs 6:6 to study its ways and be wise. This study has spurred on a new reality for me and a new style of thinking in virtually every sphere of my life. From the ant, I try to place life lessons side by side with ant operations and I can’t but admire and appreciate the sense of credibility in its pattern of existence.

As a scholar of Biblical Economics, I seek to study scriptural solutions to common economic challenges. This is much like drawing inferences from ancient history, just like it was in biblical times, to address contemporary issues.
